About Us

Discover the excellence of CV GLOBAL MARINE

CV GLOBAL MARINE is a leading marine logistics provider and ship chandler, offering comprehensive services across all Indonesian ports. Licensed for shipping logistics in Indonesia, we provide unparalleled information and services for global marine logistics, ensuring your operations run smoothly and efficiently.

With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in the marine industry. Our expertise spans from ship supplies to logistic services, always prioritizing quality and reliability.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To be the leading and most trusted marine offshore supplier in Indonesia, providing world-class services and products to our global clients.

Mission

  • Deliver high-quality marine supplies and services that exceed customer expectations
  • Maintain the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility
  • Continuously innovate and improve our processes and offerings
  • Invest in our people and foster a culture of excellence and integrity
